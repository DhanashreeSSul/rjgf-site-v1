Welcome to Raveendra Jadhav Global Foundation

Inspired by the legacy of Late Shri Ravindra Jadhav, we are dedicated to uplifting communities through compassion, integrity, and sustainable action. Join us in our journey to create a healthier, more educated, and greener world for all.

Mission

To provide essential support in healthcare, environmental conservation, and education through community-driven initiatives and partnerships.

Vision

To create a sustainable and equitable society by empowering the less privileged through healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.

Mandate

We are committed to transparency, respect, integrity, and empathy—uplifting every life we touch and fostering a sense of belonging and shared responsibility.

Core Objectives

Healthcare for All

Free medical camps, maternal & child health, nutrition, and hygiene programs.

Education Empowerment

Scholarships, digital learning, girl-child education, and skill development.

Environmental Conservation

Tree plantation, plastic-free drives, water conservation, and biodiversity protection.

Our Values

Transparency
Complete transparency in all efforts and dealings.
Respect
Respect for every individual, regardless of background.
Integrity
Upholding dignity and complying with all laws and ethics.
Empathy
Deep understanding and care for all stakeholders.
